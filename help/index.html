What is SceneDocumentor?

SceneDocumentor is - as the name indicates - a plugin for documenting your CINEMA 4D scenes.

It allows you to add text information to any object in your scene, and also make it available for people who don't have SceneDocumentor installed.

In addition to that, it can generate a simple expos√© of your scene in HTML format. The style of the document can be styled by editing the template files.

Why was it written?

SceneDocumentor was written for two main reasons: First, because there's currently no plugin for commenting and scene documentation. Second, as a donation for the Advent calendar in the german C4D forum.

Other reasons were to fill the time I spent on a train, travelling in Germany ;-), and to get to know some new aspects of the CINEMA 4D SDK.

And why OpenSource?

The community of CINEMA 4D plugin developer is quite a small one. Many people want to start writing plugins, but they have problems getting into it. A complete plugin project is always a good source of information, it shows how to approach certain common problems.

If you work on this code and improve it, it would be nice if you made it available to other people.
